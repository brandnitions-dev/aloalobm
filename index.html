// HOW A DEAL FLOWS
01 · Ad PublishedGoogle / Facebook / Social → elderly homeowner sees ad
02 · Form FillHomeowner fills form → gives consent → enters our pipeline
03 · AI CallsAI agent calls 24/7, qualifies interest, books home visit appointment
04 · Home VisitField partner visits home — explains contract, builds trust
05 · Notary SignNotary signs Leibrente / Viager contract → ownership transfers to us
06 · Fund SaleWe sell contract to investment fund → collect commission (3–4% of property value)
